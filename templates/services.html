Our Services

At Green Solucions LLP, we offer a comprehensive range of tax and visa consulting services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing personalized solutions that simplify complex processes and ensure compliance with all relevant regulations. Below are the key services we provide:

1. Tax Consulting

Our tax consulting services are tailored to help individuals and businesses navigate the complexities of tax regulations. We offer:

  • Tax Planning: We work with clients to develop effective tax strategies that minimize liabilities and maximize savings. Our proactive approach ensures that you are prepared for tax season and compliant with all regulations.
  • Tax Preparation: Our team provides comprehensive tax preparation services, ensuring that all forms are accurately completed and submitted on time. We handle both individual and business tax returns.
  • IRS Representation: If you face an audit or need to resolve issues with the IRS, our experienced professionals will represent you and advocate on your behalf, ensuring your rights are protected.
  • Tax Compliance: We help clients understand and comply with federal, state, and local tax laws, reducing the risk of penalties and ensuring peace of mind.

2. Visa Consulting

Our visa consulting services are designed to assist individuals and businesses in navigating the immigration process. We offer:

  • Visa Application Assistance: We guide clients through the visa application process, ensuring that all necessary documentation is prepared and submitted correctly. Our team is familiar with various visa categories, including work, student, and family visas.
  • Immigration Strategy Development: We work with clients to develop tailored immigration strategies that align with their personal or business goals, ensuring a smooth transition to their new status.
  • Green Card Applications: Our experts assist clients in applying for permanent residency (Green Card) through family sponsorship, employment-based petitions, or other eligible categories.
  • Compliance and Legal Support: We provide ongoing support to ensure that clients remain compliant with immigration laws and regulations, helping to avoid potential issues in the future.

3. Business Consulting

In addition to tax and visa consulting, we offer business consulting services to help companies thrive in a competitive environment. Our services include:

  • Business Formation: We assist entrepreneurs in choosing the right business structure (LLC, Corporation, etc.) and guide them through the formation process, including registration and compliance.
  • Financial Advisory: Our team provides financial analysis and advisory services to help businesses make informed decisions regarding investments, budgeting, and financial planning.
  • Regulatory Compliance: We help businesses understand and comply with industry regulations, ensuring that they operate within the legal framework and avoid penalties.
